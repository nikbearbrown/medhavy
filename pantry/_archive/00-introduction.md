## A Note about AI

This book describes Medhavy as a platform. Each chapter has its own *A note about AI* on the local platform question. The unifying principle: **the platform is opinionated; the model has no opinions of its own.** A platform thesis is a commitment about what the platform will refuse to do — a commitment the model has no basis for. The model can stress-test the thesis, surface alternatives, and pressure-test the differentiation. It cannot author the platform's identity. The chapter notes name where the model can sharpen each move and where the move belongs to the people building the platform. Build with the model. Decide the platform's purpose yourselves.
